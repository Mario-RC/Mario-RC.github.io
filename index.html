Biography

I'm a Ph.D. student at Centre for Automatic and Robotics (CAR UPM-CSIC) and a member of the Intelligent Control Group. I'm advised by Prof. Fernando Matía and Prof. Luis Fernando D'Haro.

My research interests are NLP and Dialogue Management (e.g., sentiment analysis and generative conversational agents).
