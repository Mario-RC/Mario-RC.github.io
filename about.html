About me

Hello! Welcome to my website!

Currently, I focus on developing NLP and Dialogue Management models to include emotions and personality in Dialogue Systems. I also worked on the integration of software and components for a first prototype of human-robot conversational interaction. I was previously a Research Engineer at Intelligent Control Group, where I worked on the evaluation of coherencein in Dialogue Systems. Before starting my Ph.D., I was an MSc student in Automation and Robotics, advised by Prof. Fernando Matía.
